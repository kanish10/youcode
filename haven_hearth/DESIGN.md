# Design System Document

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our design system.

## Theme Configuration

**Color Mode:** light

### Color Palette
- **Primary Color:** `#8FA89B` (A grounding, natural tone, suitable for primary actions and branding.)
- **Secondary Color:** `#A89B8F` (A warm, earthy complement, for secondary elements and accents.)
- **Tertiary Color:** `#9B8FA8` (A soft, muted accent, providing a gentle contrast.)
- **Neutral Color:** `#F5F2ED` (A light, clean background color to ensure readability and visual calm.)

### Typography
- **Headline Font:** Noto Serif (Classic and authoritative, for prominent titles.)
- **Body Font:** Manrope (Modern and highly readable, for general text.)
- **Label Font:** Manrope (Consistent with body text, ensuring clarity for UI labels.)

### Shape and Form
- **Roundedness:** Moderate (A balanced approach with slightly rounded corners, providing a friendly yet professional feel.)

### Spacing
- **Spacing:** Normal (A standard amount of whitespace, ensuring good readability and clear element separation without feeling overly sparse or cramped.)
